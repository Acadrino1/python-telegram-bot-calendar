Send this to BotFather to set up your bot commands:

/setcommands

Then paste:

start - Start the bot and see welcome message
book - Book a new appointment
myappointments - View your upcoming appointments
cancel - Cancel an appointment (use with ID)
reschedule - Reschedule an appointment
help - Show help and tips
profile - View or update your profile
availability - Set your availability (providers only)
schedule - View your schedule (providers only)
